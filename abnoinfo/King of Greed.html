{abnoName}, also known as Magical Girl, has the appearance of a dark-skinned young girl wearing a gold-coloured dress with a complicated pattern. It has long white hair that points upwards like a flame. The girl has green eyes, though they are usually closed. It is adorned in jewelry, notably a necklace and a bracelet on each arm. Additionally, the girl's hands and lower arms are black in colour.
The girl is encased inside a large golden 'egg' made of solidified amber and can be seen peacefully floating inside through a diamond-shaped window. The egg has wild, vivid patterns similar to ones on the girl's dress and makes a constant glittering sound. A small mouth is present on the upper left part of the egg, lined with sharp teeth.

After transforming, the egg morphs into a giant monster with the previously small mouth changing into a giant maw that spans the entire left side of the Abnormality. It also spawns two pairs of relatively small legs that it uses to crawl around, as well as four sets of elongated arms that shove anything in its path into its hungry maw. The girl's head hangs by a bundle of strands attached to the roof of the giant mouth. Its eyes are opened, revealing contracted pupils, with black tears streaking down its face. The white hair is let down and it constantly giggles as it moves.

In containment, {abnoName} deals 5-7 {RED} damage and produces a maximum of 22 {E-Boxes}.
Its responses to the four work orders are as follows:
{Instinct} (Common) > {Attachment} (Common) > {Repression} (Common) > {Insight} (Very Low).

Its Qliphoth Counter starts at 1.
Upon reaching 0, {abnoName} will transform and start crawling towards the other end of its containment unit. A portal will be opened up at the other end, with its connecting portal opening up at the end of a random hallway in the facility. The Abnormality will teleport through to this portal to the selected hallway where it will continue slowly crawling to the other end.
Any entity that is caught in its path will be shoved into its maw and instantly killed. Thus, employees will need to attack it from behind, and any employees in front of it will need to evacuate the hallway.
As it nears the end of the hallway, another set of portals will open up. {abnoName} will continue shambling through hallways in this manner until it is suppressed.

Managerial Guidelines
  • When the work result was {Normal}, the Qliphoth Counter decreased with a normal probability.
  • When the work result was {Bad}, the Qliphoth Counter decreased with a high probability.
  • After it escapes, {abnoName} will engulf anything that lies in its path. During suppression, pay close attention to where the Abnormality is heading.

But her desire for happiness is still within her, surfacing from time to time.

    {abnoName} appears as the third Abnormality on the {Floor of Natural Sciences}, unlocking {Awakening Intemperance}, {Awakening Road to Happiness}, and {Breakdown Greed} after its suppression.

    On the first turn, {abnoName} will be encased within its egg as Aurum Amber. This form does not act and serves as a sort of damage check. If the librarians are able to destroy the Aurum Amber on the first turn, Magical Girl will break out. Otherwise, the Magical Girl will transform into {abnoName} which can be too difficult to deal with so early on.

    Magical Girl's attacks can be quite deadly if not countered. Many of her attacks will break her opponent's dice, leaving them defenseless. Her attacks can heaviliy debilitate opponents, going so far as to even break a Speed die for the next turn.
    After 3 turns, Magical Girl will become untargetable as she prepares her mass attack, 'Road of Gold', for the next turn. She will also spawn four Brilliant Bliss which only exist for that turn, and will disappear by the next turn. Each librarian must destroy a Bliss to gain 'Fragment of Bliss' which will perfectly counter Magical Girl's mass attack the next turn.
    If all librarians are able to successfully negate the mass attack, Magical Girl will become Staggered. Otherwise, she will gain potent buffs and Stagger any librarian hit by the attack.

    Once Magical Girl reaches 50% of her HP, she will transform into {abnoName}. This form behaves similarly to Magical Girl, though its attacks now inflict {LoR SE d Bleed} and recover HP. After 3 turns, it will become untargetable and spawn four Brilliant Bliss as before. Though this time, it will use 'Road of the King' and target one librarian with it twice. The targetted librarian must destroy two Bliss to gain 2 copies of an upgraded 'Fragment of Bliss' to counter both of {abnoName}'s attacks.
    If the librarian successfully negates both attacks, {abnoName} will become Staggered. Otherwise, it will instantly kill the librarian and recover HP.
