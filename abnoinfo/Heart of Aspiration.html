{abnoName} appears as a disembodied heart with a dark red clot glowing inside of it. Despite being removed from a body, it still beats. When someone borrows the heart, the rate of its heartbeats will increase, and it will imbue the holder with increased vigour and strength.
However, if the wielder is unable to meet their cravings or aspiration, they will be driven into a violent frenzy and attack anyone on sight.

Lobotomy Corporation

{abnoName} in
Lobotomy Corporation

{abnoName} is a Tool-type Abnormality and does not produce Enkephalin. An employee can borrow the heart to boost their HP and attack speed, making it useful for carrying out suppressions.
If the employee is ordered to return the heart without attacking anything, or within 30 seconds of equipping it, they will panic. Still having the heart equipped, the panicked employee will begin indiscriminately attacking anyone they come across.

Method
  • Subjects who equip the {abnoName} will benefit from increased HP and attack speed.
  • After being ordered to return the {abnoName} without attacking, the wearer will lose their mind and have an increase in violent tendencies, attacking anything nearby.
  • If the subject is ordered to return the {abnoName} within 30 seconds after equipping it, it will lead to the same effect as above.
Log
  • A heart without an owner. It still beats even after being removed from the body.
  • When equipped, the heartrate will double, raising both vigour and strength.

Library of Ruina

{abnoName} in Library of Ruina

{abnoName} appears as the second Abnormality on the {Floor of General Works}, unlocking {Breakdown Pulsation}, {Awakening Urging}, and {Breakdown Fervent Beats} after its suppression.

In its book, {abnoName} has a completely different appearance. It is much larger, taller than the average human, and has grown arms and legs. Its left arm is a long blue vein that it uses as a whip to attack opponents. It still lack eyes, but now has a mouth lined with several small teeth. The sound of it beating can be heard throught the battle.

It is also accompanied by two Lungs of Craving. While the heart stays on the defensive, only blocking incoming attacks and building up {Light}, the Lungs will provide the main source of damage. Once the {abnoName} builds up enough {Light}, it will use 'Beats of Aspiration'. If this attack hits, it will grant 3 {LoR SE b Strength} and {LoR SE b Haste} to the Lungs. Its heartbeats will also double in speed and the screen will begin to rapidly pulse with the beats.
