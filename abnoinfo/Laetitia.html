The kid couldn't bear to leave her friends behind, she came up with a brilliant idea!

{abnoName}, also known as Wee Witch, is a doll resembling a little girl. It wears a frilled red and white lolita-style dress, with a big red bow, red boots, and a red bonnet. A pair of jingle bells are tied behind its head, hanging below its short grey hair. Being a doll, its movement is mechanical. A music box can often be heard when near it.

{abnoName}'s main desires are to make friends and spread joy and laughter. When it finds a person to be gloom, {abnoName} will gift them a hand-made heart-shaped present. However the gift secretly contains one of {abnoName}'s friends, a Wee Witch's Friend. After an amount of time, the gift will burst with a small explosion and the Friend will burst out from the the giftee's chest.
The Wee Witch's Friend is a large black spider-like monster with several eyes bulging in various directions. It has four legs each painted in a variety of colours, as well as a fifth claw-like limb. Unlike {abnoName}, the Wee Witch's Friend is hostile.

While the intention of this prank is to simply cause surprise and laughter, the bursting of the gift is usually fatal and the resulting Friend will begin attacking anyone nearby.

Lobotomy Corporation

{abnoName} in
Lobotomy Corporation

In containment, {abnoName} deals 2-4 {BLACK} damage and produces a maximum of 16 {E-Boxes}.
Its responses to the four work orders are as follows:
{Attachment} (High) > {Instinct} (Common) > {Insight} (Common) > {Repression} (Very Low).

When an employee produces a {Normal} work result after working with {abnoName}, the Abnormality will gift them a small heart-shaped present which will remain with the employee for the rest of the day. The gift will not affect the employee in anyway until that employee works with another Abnormality aside from {abnoName}.

A few seconds after beginning work with a different Abnormality, the gift will explode and the employee will immediately die. Additionally, a Wee Witch's Friend will spawn from the explosion and begin attacking other employees with {RED} damage.

It does not have a Qliphoth Counter, and does not breach containment.

Managerial Guidelines
  • When the work result was {Normal}, {abnoName} gave a gift to the employee.
  • When said employee was assigned to work with a different Abnormality instead of {abnoName}, a ███████ burst out from their body.
  • When the assignment was cancelled before the ███████ could burst out, nothing strange occurred to the employee.

    Library of Ruina

    {abnoName} in Library of Ruina

    {abnoName} appears as the fourth Abnormality on the {Floor of Literature}, unlocking {Awakening Surprise Gift}, {Breakdown Funny Prank}, and {Breakdown Friend} after its suppression.

    In the fight, {abnoName} will primarily use defensive moves which will lower the amount of damage it takes while also buffing its allies. It will also use the occasional attack which will add a 'Gift' to the target's hand if it hits. It is initially joined by four Gift-wrapped Friends which will also add copies of 'Gift' with all their attacks.

    {abnoName} focuses targets with the least amount of 'Gift', while the Gift-wrapped Friends target the ones with the most amount of 'Gift'. After 2 turns, all the Gift-wrapped Friends will use a kamikaze attack that will add 3 copies of 'Gift' to the victim's hand.

    The main threat of the fight is being overwhelmed with copies of 'Gift'. This page explodes after 2 turns, dealing 15 damage to the holder. The only way to get rid of them is by using them, though their {Light} cost is not cheap. Furthermore, everytime a 'Gift' explodes on a librarian, they will gain 1 {LoR SE d Pit-a-pat}. Once 3 stacks of this accumulate (that is, 3 gifts explode on one librarian), the librarian will instantly die.

    When a Gift-wrapped Friend dies, or when a librarian dies due to {LoR SE d Pit-a-pat}, a Wee-Witch's Friend will spawn in their place. The Wee Witch's Friends will also target the librarian with the most amount 'Gift', but insteading of adding more copies, they can remove 1 copy with 'Receive Gift'.

    Once all of {abnoName}'s allies have been defeated, it's resistances will all change to "Fatal" allowing it to be quickly dealt with. However, due to the large number of allies that must be dealt with, while also managing 'Gifts', it can be quite difficult to reach this point. {abnoName}'s defensive dice lose more Power the more 'Gifts' the attacker has. This can help the librarian suppress {abnoName} much more quickly.
