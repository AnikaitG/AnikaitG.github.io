{abnoName} is a Tool-type Abnormality and does not produce Enkephalin. An employee can borrow the notepad to boost their {Temperance}, increasing their work success rate and work speed.
If the employee is ordered to return the notepad without working on an non-Tool Abnormality, or within 30 seconds of equipping it, they will explode on the spot. Additionally, if the employee receives enough HP or SP damage while holding the notes, they will also explode and deal a large amount of {RED} damage to everyone else in the room.

Method
  • Subjects who equip the {abnoName} will benefit from a sharp increase in stats relating to {Temperance}.
  • If ordered to return the {abnoName} within 30 seconds of equipping it, the holder's skin will start to swell and their body will explode within seconds.
  • If ordered to return the {abnoName} without performing any managerial work, the holder will explode.
  • If the holder receives a certain amount of HP or SP damage, they will explode. The explosion will cover the entire room, dealing massive {RED} to all nearby.
