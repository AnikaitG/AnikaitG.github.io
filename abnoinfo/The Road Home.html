{abnoName} is a small, young girl with light brown hair and pigtails, and a pair of big round blue eyes. The Abnormality also lacks arms. It wears a black dress, a pair of silver shoes, and a big red bow in its hair.

When certain conditions are met, a small house will fall from the sky, crushing anyone below it. {abnoName} has the ability to create glowing yellow bricks which it uses to pave a road to its home. However, the Abnormality is never able to reach the house, and ends up going on an endless journey with its three friends.

Library of Ruina

{abnoName}
in Library of Ruina

{abnoName} appears as the third Abnormality on the {Floor of Social Sciences} alongside Scaredy Cat, unlocking {Breakdown Courage}, {Awakening Home}, and {Awakening A Road Walked Together} after its suppression.

In its book, {abnoName} will try to reach the Home guarded by the librarians. If it ever reaches the Home, the battle is lost. It however cannot directly go Home and must first create a path to reach it. On the first turn, it will use a Mass Attack, 'Let’s Go, Together…', targetting two random librarians and the Home. This applies {LoR SE r Yellow Brick Road} to the librarians, and creates a yellow brick road between them leading towards the Home.
The following turn it will use a copy of 'Friends! Let’s Go Home~!' against each of the targets from the previous round sequentially. This attack cannot be redirected, and will Stagger the target on hit. If the attacks hits the Home, the battle is lost. If both librarians with {LoR SE r Yellow Brick Road} are able to win against their respective clashes against the attack, the attack on Home will be cancelled.

This pattern repeats a second time after which {abnoName} will use a Mass Attack, 'You… Wicked Wizard!', time time targetting all librarians. If all librarians are able to avoid the attack, {abnoName} will become Staggered the next turn. As the Abnormality uses high-rolling Evades every turn, this is the most opportune moment to damage it.
