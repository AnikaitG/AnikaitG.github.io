The tree reaped from what it sowed.

{abnoName} appears as a glob of red sap resting within a detached tree branch. It is described as belonging to a tree found living in the barren lands of the Outskirts.
The sap contains strong healing properties and will regenerate the health of anyone who drinks it. However, drinking the sap can occasionaly cause the consumer to rapidly melt into a green substance. The remains of the victims then serve as the tree's nutrition.

{abnoName} is a Tool-type Abnormality and does not produce Enkephalin. An employee can drink the sap which will fully recover their HP and continue healing them for around a minute.
However, the sap can also cause the consumer to explode, killing them and dealing massive {WHITE} damage to everyone else in the room with them. The chance for this to occur increases as more sap is consumed.

Method
  • Subjects who consume the {abnoName} instantly recover HP. Furthermore, they will continue to recover HP over time.
  • A side effect was found from consuming the {abnoName}. Subjects who endured the side effect exploded and died within 20 to 30 seconds after consuming the {abnoName}, dealing {WHITE} damage to everyone in the same room.
  • Experiments have confirmed that there is a positive relationship between the amount of {abnoName} consumed, the amount of HP recovered from the sap, and the probability of exploding.
