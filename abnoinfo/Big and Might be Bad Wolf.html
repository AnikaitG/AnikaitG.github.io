{abnoName} is a bipedal wolf with dark grey fur. The Wolf has a friendly appearance, displaying a smile, and gently lifting its front paws which are covered in fluffy white fur. A scar is visible over its left eye.
The Wolf is often times unable to control its instincts and will occasionally swallow people whole. While a person is trapped inside, the stomach becomes bloated and the Wolf becomes visibly distressed. However, the individual can usually be freed from inside with no major consequence.

When the Wolf fully accepts its role as 'the Big Bad Wolf', it will undergo a transformation, becoming more animalistic in appearance. It becomes a quadruped with numerous scars covering its body, and its front paws grow into large claws where the fur is completely coated in blood. Its mouth bares sharp teeth and lets out a frosty breath similar to blue glow of its eyes.

In containment, {abnoName} deals 4-8 {RED} damage and produces a maximum of 22 {E-Boxes}.
Its responses to the four work orders are as follows:
{Attachment} (Common) > {Instinct} (Common) > {Insight} (Low) > {Repression} (Very Low).

When an employee causes its Qliphoth Counter to decrease, it will swallow the employee whole. This does not kill the employee and they can be rescued by performing {Instinct} work on the Abnormality. Multiple employees can be trapped in the Wolf's stomach at a time, in which case they will all be rescued together.

Its Qliphoth Counter starts at 2.
Upon reaching 0, {abnoName} transforms and breaches containment. It will immediately rush towards the main room of the nearest department which will be marked by a blue moon. Upon reaching its destination, it will let out a howl that deals {WHITE} damage to all employees in the department. If the moon was red instead of blue, its howl will also decrease the Qliphoth Counter of all Abnormalities contained in the department by 1.
After reaching the department, it will run around the department attacking targets with its claws and fangs. Once there are no entities left in the department, it will rush to a new department. Alternatively, if the Wolf sustains enough damage, it will retreat from the department, cloaking itself in the shadows to travel faster. Upon reaching its destination after skulking in the shadows, it will pause for a few seconds to catch its breath, leaving it vulnerable.

If the Wolf ever crosses paths with Little Red Riding Hooded Mercenary, or it passes by its containment unit, the Mercenary will enter an enraged state as it sets the Wolf as its target. If the Mercenary successfully suppressed the Wolf, any employeey that may be trapped in the Wolf's stomach at the time will be freed and given the unique E.G.O Gift, "Sheep's Clothing".

    {abnoName} appears as the second Abnormality on the {Floor of Language}, unlocking {Awakening Instincts}, {Breakdown Claws of Savagery}, and {Awakening Role of the Wolf} after its suppression.

    {abnoName} starts in its first form as "A Wolf". It has a fairly simple behaviour where it attempts to inflict {LoR SE d Bleed} on its opponents. Every 2 turns it uses 'I’ll Gobble You Up!!' which upon hitting a target will cause them to be swallowed by the Wolf. Once the Wolf has swallowed a librarian, all of its Stagger resistances will be set to "Fatal" the next turn. If the remaining librarians successfully Stagger the Wolf on this turn, the librarian will be vomitted out, losing a quarter of their max health.

    If the Wolf is not Staggered after it swallows a librarian, it will eat them (removing them from the battle) and transform. The transformation will also occur after the Wolf reaches half its health, and it's health will not go under 30% before the transformation takes place to prevent it from being skipped.

    After transforming, the Wolf uses more powerful attacks to inflict {LoR SE d Bleed} and recover HP. It also uses Counter Block dice which can punish one-sided attacks, granting it {LoR SE b Strength} the next turn. After losing a quarter of the HP it had after transforming, the Wolf will hide itself in the shadows. While it is in this 'Skulking in the Shadows' state, it will target only 1 librarian, and only this librarian will be able to target the Wolf. This state lasts for 2 turns after which the Wolf will have all its resistances set to "Fatal" for one turn as it recovers from skulking in the shadows.
