{abnoName} normally takes the form of a small timid cat with tan fur. It has huge teary eyes and is constantly shaking in fear, with the fur on its back raised. When in the presence of one of its three friends, its nature switches from cowardly to courageous and it transforms.

After transforming it becomes a large manticore with red fur and a blue mane. It has red eyes and bares sharp teeth. While it mainly uses its claws to attack opponents, it can also shoot poison from its scorpion-like tail. While this form is hostile and deadly, it will revert to its courageous form when it is separated from its friends.

{abnoName} appears as the third Abnormality on the {Floor of Social Sciences} alongside The Road Home, unlocking {Breakdown Courage}, {Awakening Home}, and {Awakening A Road Walked Together} after its suppression.

In the Road Home's book, {abnoName} serves as the main offensive force, relentlessly attacking the librarians while the Road Home tries to reach home which will result in the battle being lost. Not only are its attacks relatively high-rolling, but it is also immune to all forms of damage while the Road Home is present.

On the turn where the Road Home uses 'Let’s Go, Together…', {abnoName} will gain the {LoR SE r Courage} effect. This effect gives {abnoName} 1 {LoR SE b Strength} next turn for each time the Road Home is attacked.
When the Road Home is Staggered, it will use 'Alertness' which can be quite potent due to the amount of {LoR SE d Fragile} it can inflict.

After the Road Home is defeated and {abnoName} is all alone, it will transform into its cowardly form. Upon doing so, it's maximum HP drastically decreases and it becomes "Fatal" to all damage. It will also only use 'Nervous as a Cat' from this point onwards, meaning it can no longer deal any damage.
