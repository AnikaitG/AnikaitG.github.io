{abnoName} has the appearance of a young girl that has been charred beyond recognition. Its entire body, including its hair and pigtails, have a blackened, burnt appearance. Its legs are short and thin and it lacks arms.

A large matchstick is impaled through its torso. The matchstick is perpetually lit and appears to never extinguish.

{abnoName} is capable of self-destructing. It typically picks a target and heads toward them, ignoring any one else in its path. It is usually heard sobbing during this process. Once it reaches its target, it explodes in a fiery blast killing the target as well as anyone else in the vicinity.

Abnormality Story

An Abnormality in the form of a burnt girl. Even though there's nothing left to burn, the fire engulfing her does not extinguish.

A matchstick has impaled the girl's body like a stake. The match is always lit, while the Abnormality shows no signs of distress or activity. Because of this, some employees speculate that the actual Abnormality is the match and not the girl. However, there have recently been reports of the scorched girl weeping.

    Library of Ruina

    {abnoName} in Library of Ruina

    {abnoName} appears as the first Abnormality on the {Floor of History}, unlocking {Awakening Ashes}, {Breakdown Footfalls}, and {Breakdown Matchlight} after its suppression.

    In its book, {abnoName} is accompanied by two Last Match Flames which appear as burnt out matchsticks with stick-like arms and legs. The Last Match Flames are the main enemies during the battle, inflicting {LoR SE d Burn} as well as evading attacks to stall out turns while {abnoName} builds up {Light}.

    After {abnoName} fully builds up {Light}, it uses the skill 'Fourth Match Flame', approaching the target and exploding causing an instant death in most cases. This attack deals no damage to {abnoName} and, in the event the battle continues, it returns to building up {Light}.

    Every time a Last Match Flame is deafeated, {abnoName} loses all it's {Light}, half its health and gets Staggered. Defeating both matches leads to the Abnormality's suppresion.
