{abnoName} is a small, white cleaning robot. It has an oval-shaped bodies with two red eyes and a small smile. When in an idle state, the body is supported by a pair of metal legs. It has a variety of functions installed in its body designed to help out a household with their daily chores.

When it detects a bad mood, it will enter cleaning mode. Due to a technical malfunction, {abnoName} will pull out sharp blades instead of cleaning supplies when it enters cleaning mode. Its legs further extend, and an additional pair also extends out from the top of its body. Each of the four legs is tipped with a sharp, blood-stained blade.

As part of its 'cleaning', {abnoName} will spin around baring its blades, slicing anything in its path. Due to its pattern recognition system, it understands human screams as a sign of a job well done which encourages it to continue 'cleaning'.

Abnormality Story

A white cleaning robot, with distinct red eyes that look like a pair of buttons. It always keeps its surroundings neat and tidy. "Manufactured by X Corp." is written on its back.

It was originally a mass produced helper robot. “An innovative invention that can clean, cook, and even serve as light security, this robot is all you need to keep your home safe and in tiptop condition!”

It recognises bad moods as a "dirty environment" and will enter cleaning mode. When it goes into cleaning mode it will alert its status by saying “Contamination detected. Commencing cleaning.” However, instead of cleaning supplies, it deploys sharp instruments that were installed in its body, like knives and saws, to carry out the cleaning.
Apparently, these tools were supposed to be used for cutting and preparing meat, but the commands registered to the tools for cooking and cleaning had been 'accidentally' switched. No official comment has been made regarding this.

Due to its faulty pattern recognition and analysis feature, it has determined that the screaming people make after it cleans is a sign of a job well done, and a cue to continue its cleaning.
