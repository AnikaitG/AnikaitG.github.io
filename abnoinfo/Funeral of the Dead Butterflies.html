What happens when people die?

{abnoName} is a monochrome humanoid with a butterfly for a head. It is dressed in formal attire which is primarily black in colour save for a white undershirt. The butterfly that serves as its head is white with black markings, and has three layers of wings.

Funeral possesses five arms. While its main pair of arms are usually tucked into the pockets of its jacket, its secondary pair of arms which are much longer carry a coffin behind the Abnormality's back. A fifth arm emerges from its chest, right under the collar of the undershirt.

The coffin carried by Funeral is black with a butterfly similar to the Abnormality's head depicted on it. The coffin carries a large number of butterflies that the Abnormality attacks with, to give people the eternal rest that they deserve.

Lobotomy Corporation

{abnoName}
in Lobotomy Corporation

In containment, {abnoName} deals 4-6 {WHITE} damage and produces a maximum of 16 {E-Boxes}.
Its responses to the four work orders are as follows:
{Repression} (High) > {Insight} (Common) > {Instinct} (Very Low) > {Attachment} (Very Low).
Its preferences are slightly different for lower level agents, where {Repression} has a Very Low success rate while {Instinct} offers a Common success rate.

Its Qliphoth Counter starts at 2.
Upon reaching 0, {abnoName} breaches containment. It will roam around the facility, 'firing' butterflies from its fingers at its targets (described as making them bloom out of thin air). It will also occasionally set down its coffin in front of it and open it, causing a seemingly endless amount of butterflies to fly out. These butterflies reach all the way to the end of the room, causing continuous {WHITE} damage to anyone caught in the attack.

Managerial Guidelines
  • When an employee with {Justice} Level 2 or below finished the work, the Qliphoth Counter decreased.
  • When an employee with {Fortitude} Level 4 or above finished the work, the Qliphoth Counter decreased.
  • When the work result was {Bad}, the Qliphoth Counter decreased with a high probability.

    Library of Ruina

    {abnoName}
    in Library of Ruina

    {abnoName} appears as the fourth Abnormality on the {Floor of Technology}, unlocking {Awakening Eternal Rest}, {Awakening Lament}, and {Awakening Coffin} after its suppression.

    In its book, {abnoName} is joined by four Kaleidoscopes of Butterflies, each of which is a group of three white butterflies. Funeral's strategy focuses on Staggering its opponents. When a librarian is Staggered, Funeral will put down its coffin in front of it, preparing its Mass Attack, 'Lament'. This attack will instantly kill any Staggered librarians on hit.

    The Kaleidoscopes always target the librarian with the least health, inflicting potent stat downgrades and additional Stagger damage. Funeral will also target the weakest librarian and will try to {LoR SE d Seal} their Speed dice to forcefully Stagger them.
    If these attacks are successfully beaten in a clash, they will instead {LoR SE d Seal} Funeral's Speed dice. If both of its Speed dice are Sealed, Funeral will become Staggered itself.
