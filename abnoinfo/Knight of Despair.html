{abnoName}, also known as Magical Girl, has the appearance of a pale-skinned girl wearing a long blue dress that reaches the floor. Both its hair and dress are long and blue, gradually shifting to black near the end. Stars and constellations can be seen inprinted on both. The Abnormality has long slender arms, clasped together that turn black halfway down the upper arm. Half of its face is black and faceless with spikes protruding from the side. The other half of the face carries a sad expression; a small frown, a close eye, and a small black tear on the cheek. The symbol of a spade in embroidered at the top of its dress.

The Abnormality has a desire to protect others. It will impart to them its blessing to protect them from harm. However, if the one it swore to protect falls, despite the blessing, {abnoName} will become overcome with grief and undergo a transformation.
It will don its armour, covering its chest and arms, as jagged black spikes sprout out from the head, the upper arms, and the dress. Its eye will open revealing an empty socket with tears spilling out. It will summon a number of swords which will pierce all those it failed to protect.

In containment, {abnoName} deals 4-6 {WHITE} damage and produces a maximum of 22 {E-Boxes}.
Its responses to the four work orders are as follows:
{Attachment} (High) > {Insight} (Common) > {Repression} (Low) > {Instinct} (Very Low).

When an employee achieves a {Good} work result, they will receive {abnoName}'s blessing. This will half all incoming damage, except {PALE} damage which will instead be doubled. It will also prevent the employee from performing any further work for the rest day. Only one employee can have the blessing at a time.

It does not have a Qliphoth Counter, but will breach containment when the blessed employee dies or panics. It will summon four swords and teleport to different rooms in the faciity, hurling the swords at employees and inflicting {PALE} damage. During its escape, it can be heard weeping loudly.

Managerial Guidelines
  • The first employee who achieved a {Good} work result with {abnoName} received its blessing.
  • The blessed employee could only perform suppressions. Thanks to the blessing, {RED}, {WHITE}, and {BLACK} damage dealt to them was halved. However, {PALE} damage was doubled.
  • The blessing dissipated when the blessed employee either died or panicked. After the blessing was gone, a new employee could receive it.
  • When the blessed employee panicked or died, {abnoName} escaped from the containment unit.
Abnormality Story

An Abnormality of despair that has gone through a repeated cycle of trust and betrayal, leaving only behind black tears that express its emotion.

She was a former hero, a guardian of justice in the past. She dedicated her life to protecting the king, the state, the city, and those in need.
A knight has to uphold the laws of God and be in pursuit of valour, chivalry, and justice. But after a long history filled with conflict, the knight realized that nothing was truly upheld. The truth changed the people.
The one who fought for justice became evil when they realized there was no evil left in the world. The one who dreamed of a world of peace turned to greed and began devouring everything.
Like them, many other things that once were thought would never change became altered and disappeared.

Who said a knight never cries? Have you ever seen tears brought on from despair, not sadness?
The endless stream of grief turned the eye sockets into empty husks. Black diamonds sometimes fall from inside like ash.
As if its will to defend still remains, it is obsessed with protecting even while imprisoned. All that remains is the hollow pride of a weathered knight. The sight of the hero that cannot let go of the past fills one with pity.

When justice turns its back once more, several dozen blades will linger on without a purpose. In shame of failing once again to protect, in rage against the world, the blades will eventually point towards those she could not protect.

The knight who wanted to redeem herself by saving others is now called the {abnoName}; forgotten from the minds of the people, just like the others.

    Library of Ruina

    {abnoName} in Library of Ruina
    {abnoName} after transforming
    ❮ ❯

    {abnoName} appears as the second Abnormality on the {Floor of Natural Sciences}, unlocking {Breakdown Blessing}, {Breakdown Despair}, and {Awakening Blades} after its suppression.

    In its book, {abnoName} is joined by 3 Swords of a Forgotten Knight. The Knight itself will be untargetable and will not act with the Swords fighting in its stead. Two of the Swords will use simple attacks and attempt to inflict {LoR SE d Fragile}.
    One Sword will receive the {LoR SE r Tears} effect from the Knight which will change its Slash and Blunt resistances to "Ineffective" and its Pierce resistance to "Fatal". It will also gain an extra Speed die and deal percentage-based damage to targets. After 3 turns, the {LoR SE r Tears} effect will expire and be reapplied to another Sword.

    The Swords cannot be killed and will instead enter a suspension state for 2 turns after taking lethal damage. If the Sword with {LoR SE r Tears} takes lethal damage, {abnoName} will fall into a Despair state and transform. All 3 Swords will fully recover and use 'Heart-piercing Sword', 'Heart-breaking Sword', and 'Heart-tearing Sword' between them.
    If a Sword fails to deal damage to its target, it will instead impale {abnoName} and deal damage to it. Once {abnoName} is impaled by all 3 Swords, the screen will crack as it becomes Staggered and targettable.
