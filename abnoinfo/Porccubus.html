{abnoName} is a plant-like creature with a thick serpentine body resembling a plant's stem. Several leaf-like spines are present on its body. The Abnormality possesses a red flower for a head, with a yellow centre resembling a nose, a pair of black eyes, and a pair of ear-like petals resembling those of a dog's.

The tail is connected to a lightweight rubber ball covered in thorns. The thorns secrete a powder that induces pleasure into anyone that touches them. The pleasure received from the thorns is so great that it can cause the subject's blood to rush to their head, causing it to burst.

In containment, {abnoName} deals 1-5 {BLACK} damage and produces a maximum of 18 {E-Boxes}.
Its responses to the four work orders are as follows:
{Instinct} (High) > {Attachment} (Common) > {Insight} (Common) > {Repression} (Low).

Employees with low {Temperance} are susceptible to being instantly killed by {abnoName} if they produce a {Good} work result, being easy prey to the Abnormality's pleasure-inducing thorns.

Its Qliphoth Counter starts at 2.
Upon reaching 0, {abnoName} breaches containment. It will teleport to a random corridor in the facility where it will remain in a fixed position. {abnoName} is immune to ranged attacks, meaning employees have to be within its attacking range to deal damage to it. {abnoName} deals {WHITE} damage with its attacks, dealing increased damage to employees with low {Temperance}. When an employee panics due to the Abnormality's attacks, they will begin laughing until their head bursts from the pleasure.
