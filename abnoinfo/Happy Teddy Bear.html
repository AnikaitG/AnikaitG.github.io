{abnoName} is a large, brown teddy bear that is worn-out from years of neglect. When standing up it is taller than the average human, though it is often seen sitting and slouching. Its right ear is nibbled, and its right eye is missing with a big hole left in its place. Stuffing is seen peeking out from seams at multiple points. It also wears a tattered, green bowtie.

{abnoName} is nostalgic for the old days, when it was loved by its previous owner and longs for affection. When it feels that it has found a new owner that loves it, Teddy will hug them tightly refusing to let go in fear of being abandoned again. This usually leads to target dying of asphyxiation.

In containment, {abnoName} deals 2-4 {WHITE} damage and produces a maximum of 15 {E-Boxes}.
Its responses to the four work orders are as follows:
{Attachment} (High) > {Repression} (Common) > {Insight} (Common) > {Instinct} (Very Low).
It perfers work performed by lower level agents.

If the same employee enters Teddy's containment unit two times in a row, they receive a deathly embrace from the Abnormality which instantly kills them before they can start the work. In order to avoid this, employee should be assigned to work with {abnoName} in an alternating pattern.

It does not have a Qliphoth Counter, and does not breach containment.

Abnormality Story

An Abnormality that resembles a large, raggedy teddy bear. It's missing some fur, and only has one eye.

Judging from the worn ribbon around its neck, its former owner must have loved the bear very much. There's a name written on it, but the ribbon is too tattered to make it out. It seems the bear knows that it has been abandoned, and appears dejected.

    Library of Ruina

    {abnoName} in Library of Ruina

    {abnoName} appears as the second Abnormality on the {Floor of History}, unlocking {Awakening Nostalgic Embrace of the Old Days}, {Awakening Happy Memories}, and {Breakdown Display of Affection} after its suppression.

    In its combat encounter, {abnoName} is no longer slouching on the floor but instead is standing up, eager to 'play' with the librarians. Whenever a librarian clashes with {abnoName}, it will lower the Cost of 'Nostalgic Embrace of the Old Days' regardless of whether it won or lost the clash. This is marked by hearts appearing above its head the next turn accompanied by the sound of a music box.

    As 'Nostalgic Embrace of the Old Days' starts with a Cost of 5 {Light} and {abnoName} only has a maximum of 2 {Light}, it will be unable to use this attack until it has clashed at least 3 times. It is best to ignore Teddy's attacks, only clashing when absolutely necessary. Due to its high Speed its attacks cannot be redirected, meaning the targetted librarian will need to to endure the hits while their ally performs one-sided attacks against the teddy.
