{abnoName} has the appearance of an industrial meat grinder. It is cuboidal in shape, and supported by four legs. Two funnels are present, one coming out of the machine's right side and the other from the top on the left. The upper half of the machine is a lid which can open up perpendicular to the bottom half, along a visible circular black joint in the middle-left of the machine's body. The interior of the machine is lined with several saw blades.

When approaching {abnoName}, an individual may feel a temptation to throw themselves into the machine. If they do so, the lid will close behind them and the machine will begin operation. While cutting up the individual inside, {abnoName} will produce music using the victim as its instrument. This music is highly addictive and will cause listeners to become enamoured by it. During the machine's operation, the blood of the victim is spewed out from the funnels. Once the machine fully digests its meal, there will be little to no trace of its victim.

Individuals who are addicted to {abnoName}'s music will yearn to hear it again, and will attack others with the intent to feed them to the machine.

Abnormality Story

The machine has the shape of a long cuboid. At first glance it may look like a simple industrial grinder, but the existence of its own "will" fulfills the criteria for it to be an Abnormality.

When approached (further analysis is required to determine the actual perimeter) or touched, the employee will become delirious and throw themselves into the Abnormality. Afterwards, the machine's ventilation fan will shut off and it will begin operation. This takes approximately 5 minutes.

After a few experiments, it was determined that the Abnormality desires to produce "music" from this interaction.

There is no detailed research of the Abnormality's "music". However, it is theorised that the music has a special characteristic that attratcs people based on their specific behaviour or psychological records. If an employee has been determined to be addicted to the music, they must be treated immediately to prevent any collateral damage.
