{abnoName} is a large mechanical humanoid made entirely of metal. Its exterior is covered in moss and coated with dried bloodstains and rust. Cogwheels can be seen connecting its joints and two exhaust pipes can be seen protruding from its back. It has a cylindrical head that somewhat resembles a knight's helmet. There is a hatch present on its chest, opened up to reveal an empty cavity. It also wields a giant bloodied axe in one of its hands.

The Woodsman is usally seen kneeling, remaining completely stationary. The arm wielding the axe is raised as if its ready to strike at any given moment. When it detects a person with a warm heart, its slams its axe down on them. It will then take their heart and stuff it into the empty cavity in its chest.

Abnormality Story

It is a mechanical Abnormality with a humanoid body made entirely out of metal. It weilds a giant axe in one hand but shows no signs of movement, as if it were a statue.

The friends were incomplete in one way or another, so they helped each other to fill what they were lacking and embarked on a journey together to make their wishes come true. The Scarecrow wanted a brain, the Tin Woodsman wanted a heart, the Cowardly Lion wanted courage, and Dorothy wanted to go back home. However, the one they thought to be a great wizard gave the Woodsman a worthless lead heart.

“But you're a machine. That means you were built to serve humans. Don't you understand? A machine doesn't need a heart. Thank you for coming all this way though, take this as compensation. Be sure to tell everyone waiting for you that your wish came true.”
Some left satisfied, but he could not see his friends again. The hard, cold lead made him sad.

The Woodsman disagreed. He swung his axe in anger and cut the wizard's chest. His heart was warm and was beating vigorously. He placed the lead heart in the wizard's chest and began to laught at the sight. He felt a pleasure he had never felt before.

Now the Woodsman shoves a mass of hearts into his chest. But no matter how many hearts he has, it will never be enough. Once called the Woodsman, he now cuts down people instead of trees. This is a forest full of hearts. No matter how many he cuts down, the forest remains dense. Those who are kind-hearted will also have warm hearts.

If the Woodsman loses his hearts, he will fall to his knees in despair, clutching his empty chest. As if begging you to not take the only thing it has left.

The librarians must try to use up as much {Light} as possible as the Woodsman will gain Power equal to their remaining {Light} when attacking them.

    {LoR SE b Warm Heart} will increase the maximum roll for the Woodsman's dice. It will gain 3 stacks of {LoR SE b Warm Heart} and heal 20% of its HP every time it kills a character. The amount of {LoR SE b Warm Heart} the Woodsman has can be reduced by winning certain clashes. Once all stacks of {LoR SE b Warm Heart} have been depleted, the Woodsman will become Staggered the following turn.
