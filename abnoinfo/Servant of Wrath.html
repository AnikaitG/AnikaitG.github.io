{abnoName}, also known as Magical Girl, has the appearance of a small girl with short green hair. It wears a green suit with bright green gems embedded in the arms, on a belt around the waist, on its shoes, and at the base of its cravat. It has red eyes, though they are rarely seen as the Abnormality is almost always seen wearing a blindfold that completely obscures its eyes.
It wields a small green hammer which creates a green corrosive sludge to fight off evil-doers. {abnoName} is also shown to be able to float in the air and this is usually how it moves around.

The Servant possesses a strong desire to uphold justice balance. Despite this, it also likes making friends and will usually become attached a particular companion. However, once it feels that its principles have been broken by not treating all equally, it will enter a state of distress. During this state, it will turn from green to yellow, and finally into red. It also sheds tears of acid which slowly eat away at its blindfold. Eventually, it will complete its transformation into its second form which is highly aggressive and fueled by rage.

After transforming, {abnoName} takes the appearance of a red-coloured bipedal beast. It is larger and more muscular in stature, losing the ability to float. Its head is animal-like with a snout and its blindfold, now torn in two, hangs from both its eyes. The only attire it wears in this form is a cravat around its neck and a pair of olive green shorts. Its feet are hooved while its hands are replaced by big red hammers. Acidic sludge leaks from the ends of its hammers and mouth. A single large eye is present in the centre of the torso, surrounded by six red gems.
The Servant's transformation is accompanied by its companion transforming into the Hermit of the Azure Forest.

The source of the Servant's distress is its history with the Hermit, an enemy of its realm. It secretely became friends with the Hermit, sharing its knowledge with it. The Hermit eneded up betraying the Servant, bringing countless minions to destroy the Servant's world.

{abnoName} appears as the fourth Abnormality on the {Floor of Natural Sciences}, unlocking {Breakdown Wrath}, {Awakening Companion}, and {Awakening Acidic Sludge} after its suppression.

In its book, {abnoName} acts as an ally as its faces off against the Hermit which itself is joined by 3 Hermit's Staves. The Servant will always prioritize the Staves, only attacking the Hermit when there are no Staves left. In turn, the Staves will always target the Servant. The Hermit usually targets the librarians, only targetting the Servant when all of its Staves have been defeated (and during mass attacks as well).
The Hermit can only take fatal damage from the Servant, healing itself considerably if ending a turn at 30- HP. This also means the battle cannot be won if the Servant is ever defeated.

The Servant uses powerful attacks that inflict {SE LoR d Erosion}. It will even damage itself to gain {LoR SE b Strength} the next turn. While the Staves' attacks are comparatively weaker, the Hermit can buff and heal with its pages. After taking too much damage, the Servant will use a mass attack which will target all other characters including the librarians.

After all Staves are defeated, the Servant will use its mass attack, 'EMBODIMENTS OF EVIL!!!!' and target its other attacks against the Hermit.
The Hermit will use 3 copies of 'Dear Companion…!', targetting the Servant with all three. This attack will Stagger the target and inflict them with {LoR SE r Cane}. Its Speed will also become fixed at 1, allowing the librarians to redirect its attacks to protect the Servant.
The following turn, the Hermit will use 3 copies of 'You Shall Fall…!' to follow up its previous attacks. If the Hermit survives to the next turn, it will use a mass attack to summon 3 more Staves.

Once the Servant defeats the Hermit, it will return to its Magical Girl form and the suppression will end in success.
