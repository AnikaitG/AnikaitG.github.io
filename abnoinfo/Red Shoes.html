{abnoName} are a pair of elegant women's shoes made of a red enameled leather that glistens in the light. People who gaze at the shoes are filled with an overwhelming urge to put them on. If someone puts them on, sharp teeth will extend from the interior of the shoes and bite into the ankles of the wearer, latching on to them.

After wearing the shoes, the wearer will experience a sharp increase in all their desires, including those which they would not normally act upon. Due to an increase in aggression, these desires are usually carried out in a violent manner.

The shoes are incredible difficult to remove on account of the teeth digging into their victim's ankles. This often leads to an individual needing to be dismember the wearer's feet to remove the shoes; whether so as to suppress them, or to take the shoes for themselves. The shoes are also known to be able to cut themselves of the ankles of their host to run away should they need to.

Abnormality Story

The Abnormality takes the form of a pair of elegant red women's shoes. The design is antique, but there's no telling where or how they were made.

When observing the shoes, a strong urge to try them on is induced. The changes that occur from wearing the Abnormality are first visible in the eyes of the wearer.

The next observable change is that the wearer is unwilling to remove the shoes. They will also act upon any and all desires they may have. If someone attempts to hamper them from acting on their desires or tries to remove the shoes, the shoes will take control of its host which sometimes leads to murder.

It is unknown whether the host's will is involved in the process, but considering the sharp increase of desire and aggression, the host's primal urges seem to remain intact.
