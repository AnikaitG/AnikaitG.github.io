Abnormality Story

The Abnormality takes the form of a giant spider, guarding its domain in darkness. Small spiderlings skitter around the floor while unidentified wires hang from the ceiling. It's difficult to make out the Abnormality as the cables running through the ceiling of the containment unit are damaged, denying illuination.

Our proud employee Peter entered the Abnormality's containment unit and saw the spiderlings scattering away from the light that came through the door. As soon as Peter squashed one of the spiderlings, a horrible thing occurred.
A giant spider with many eyes descended from the ceiling and took Peter back up with it in a matter of seconds. The spider gleefully wove him into a cocoon. The cocooned employee hanging from the ceiling of the containment unit will make a healthy meal for the spider's offspring.

Unsurprisingly, not a single employee volunteered to retrieve the corpse of their cocooned colleague.

Most people don't realise, but the victims trapped inside cocoons are not dead. They're kept fresh for later consumption, as they slowly die within. Spider venom contains a special toxin that permanently damages the heart, eventually leading to death. The victim trapped inside the cocoon has no choice but to wait in the dark for the spiderlings to consume them, listening to the slow beating of their own heart.
