{abnoName} is a tall pink entity resembling a llama, made from ceramic. It has six long legs, and a long, thick neck with a human head. The neck is covered in a bush of bright green leaves. The Abnormality is wearing a pink wide-brim hat with pink flowers dangling along the brim. {abnoName} has no eyes, and it eye sockets are filled with green flowers. Cracks are present in the body and legs, but they are filled by pink flowers. A mass of pink flowers are also seen spilling out from its mouth. Due to the abundance of flower, {abnoName} gives off a strong lavender smell.

{abnoName} is described to be a doll that wished to be human. But as it was born from the earth, it now wishes to return to the soil. Pink flowers violently bloom inside of its victims.

Lobotomy Corporation

{abnoName} in
Lobotomy Corporation

In containment, {abnoName} deals 4-6 {WHITE} damage and produces a maximum of 22 {E-Boxes}.
Its responses to the four work orders are as follows:
{Instinct} (High) > {Insight} (Common) > {Attachment} (Low) > {Repression} (Very Low).
The work success rate is 0% for low level employees, regardless of the work order. The success rate for {Instinct} and {Insight} increases with employee level, while it decreases for {Attachment}.

Its Qliphoth Counter starts at 1.
Upon reaching 0, {abnoName} will become obscured by a curtain of flowers and teleport out of its containment unit and into a random hallway. It will remain stationary until another entity enters the hallway. Once this happens, a pink petal will bloom behind {abnoName} every few seconds. Once 3 petals have bloomed, {abnoName} will deal massive {WHITE} damage to everything in the hallway and teleport to a different one.
If an employee's SP drops to 0 from this attack, they will die.

Abnormality Story

Its surface is smooth like ceramic. Its eye sockets are empty. The openings are instead filled with growing plants.

Alriune, a doll yearned to be human. With her soulful eyes, it was adored by all.

Alriune, abandoned in a dark forest. Her glistening eyes stolen by crows. Her mind, life, and heart slowly withered away.

Alriune, seeing all even without eyes, a human yearned to be a doll. With her hope to return to dust, it will return to the grave with everything that desires to live.

    Library of Ruina

    {abnoName} in Library of Ruina

    {abnoName} appears as the fourth Abnormality on the {Floor of Art}, unlocking {Breakdown Petals}, {Breakdown Autumn's Passing}, and {Awakening Spring's Genesis} after its suppression.

    In its book, {abnoName} has a more humanoid appearance. The lower half of its body resembles a large gown, with pink flowers spilling out of several big cracks. Pink flowers also cover its four arms and hang like a curtain from its hat, completely obscuring its hat. {abnoName} is also joined by four entities, each called 'One Who Emerged from the Earth', which represent its victims.

    The Ones Who Emerged from the Earth endure all physical attacks, and are immune to both {LoR SE d Burn} and {LoR SE d Bleed}. They however take normal Stagger damage and immediately die upon becoming Staggered. Similarly, physical attacks are ineffective against {abnoName}. While Staggering {abnoName} will not instantly kill it, it will still lose 40 HP and all librarians will recover 50% of their Stagger Resist.
    All enemies only deal Stagger damage through their attacks. Whenever a librarian gets Staggered, they immediately die and turn into One Who Emerged from the Earth the following turn.

    On the first turn, {abnoName} will use 'Winter's Inception' to inflict {LoR SE r Laurel Wreath} on 1 target. {abnoName} will only receive damage from the librarian with the {LoR SE r Laurel Wreath} status. The other librarians must protect this librarian from the Ones Who Emerged from the Earth's attacks. These attacks will change the target's Stagger resistances to "Fatal", and can even {LoR SE d Immobilize} the target, so they must be avoided at all costs.
    All enemies can additionally inflict {LoR SE d Aroma} which can deal heavy damage to the target's Stagger Resist.

    Starting from the second turn, a petal will bloom behind {abnoName} at the start of each turn. Once three petals have accumulated, it uses its mass attack, 'Magnificent End'. This countdown can be reset by Staggering {abnoName}. After recovering from Stagger, or after the librarian with the {LoR SE r Laurel Wreath} is killed, {abnoName} will select a new librarian to receive the {LoR SE r Laurel Wreath}.
